Dynatrace Acronyms Glossary

DEM
Digital Experience Monitoring, Gartner defines it as availability and performance monitoring discipline that supports the optimization of the operational experience and behavior of a digital agent, human or machine, as it interacts with enterprise applications and services. it consists of two monitorings:
  1. RUM
  2. Synthetic monitoring
RUM
Real User Monitoring, gives the power to know your customers by providing performance analysis in real time. This includes all user actions taken and how the various actions impact performance.
